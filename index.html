Becoming a Nutritionist

  1. Decide whether you want to be a nutritionist or a registered dietitian.
  2. Earn a bachelor's degree in a health-related field.
  3. Further your education with a master's degree or doctoral program in nutrition.
  4. Gain experience through an internship.
  5. Learn about your state's certification requirements.
  6. Obtain a recognized nutritionist certification.
  7. Get certified as a registered dietitian nutritionist.
  8. Maintain your certification.
